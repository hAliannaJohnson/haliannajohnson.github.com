Adventure Awaits!

Welcome heroic traveler to the village of Heimdale located in the mystical kingdom of Brightdom. You make your way in the world by taking care of quests, both big and small, for the townsfolk of Brightdom’s many villages. As you look at the quest postings on the wall of the Dire Wolf Tavern, you see that there are three quests to choose from.

    Which quest do you choose?

  • Find the mayor’s daughter.
  • Find the farmer’s prized chicken.
  • Gather ingredients for the herbalist.
